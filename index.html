AI Literacy Levels

Level 1: Understand AI – Basic understanding of AI concepts, machine learning, neural networks, and foundation knowledge of AI applications in healthcare and TCM.

Level 2: Use and Apply AI – Practical application of AI tools in clinical settings, implementation of AI-assisted diagnostics and treatment planning.

Level 3: Analyze and Evaluate AI – Critical evaluation of AI-generated recommendations, assessment of AI tools and their impact on clinical practice.

Level 4: Create AI – Development of new AI applications for TCM, leading AI research initiatives, and innovation in AI-driven healthcare solutions.
